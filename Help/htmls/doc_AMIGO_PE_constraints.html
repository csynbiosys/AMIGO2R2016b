AMIGO_PE enables the possibility of solving the parameter estimation problem with general non-linear constraints. Constraints may be imposed over states in the model or over parameters. States related oath constraints and point constraints (except for end-point constraints) will be handled by forcing an integral measure of their violation to a value close to zero (violation tolerance). This will imply a modification of the model to incorporate constraint violation over time or at specific times.
